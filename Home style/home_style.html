ABOUT

About the Company Origin



Hardy Peterson

Founder

Home Style was founded in 1998 by Hardy Peterson and Dake Smith while they were Ph.D. students at Stanford University in California. Together they own about 14 percent of its shares and control 56 percent of the stockholder voting power through supervoting stock. They incorporated Home Style as a privately held company on September 4, 1998.

An initial public offering (IPO) took place on August 19, 2004, and Home Style moved to its headquarters in Mountain View, California, nicknamed the HoS. In August 2015, Home Style announced plans to reorganize its various interests as a conglomerate called Alphabet Inc.
